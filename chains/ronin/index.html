Introducing RONIN:

The Future of
Gaming Blockchains

Ronin is a battle-tested, EVM-compatible blockchain specifically forged for gaming by Sky Mavis, the creators of Axie Infinity. Ronin has processed billions in NFT volumes and optimized for near-instant transactions and negligible fees, making it the leading choice for Web3 games. With Ronin, developers can create player-owned economies and scale their games to accommodate millions of daily users.

  • Proven Scalability

    Ronin is the only blockchain proven to scale a single game, Axie Infinity, to millions of daily active users, processing over $4 billion in NFT volumes.

  • Gaming-Centric Optimization

    Ronin eliminates the bloat found in other blockchains, ensuring minimal spam and high uptime for games, making it a perfect platform for high-quality gaming apps.

  • Sky Mavis Expertise

    As the creators of Axie Infinity, Sky Mavis brings deep experience in product development, community building, and go-to-market strategies, offering advisory support and grants to developers.

  • Enhanced Security

    Ronin employs rigorous security measures and a Delegated Proof of Stake (DPoS) consensus to ensure network security, with a track record of overcoming challenges like the Ronin Validator Breach.
